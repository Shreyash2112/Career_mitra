Civil Services

Civil Services are designed to prepare students for competitive examinations conducted by government bodies for recruitment into various civil services positions. These courses focus on subjects such as general knowledge, current affairs, aptitude, and specific topics relevant to the civil services exams. Civil service, the body of government officials who are employed in civil occupations that are neither political nor judicial.

Top Academy

Rau's IAS Study Circle, Delhi

Rau's IAS Study Circle is one of the oldest and most renowned institutes for civil services coaching in India. Known for its experienced faculty, comprehensive study materials, regular test series, and personalized mentoring. Offers coaching for all stages of the UPSC Civil Services Examination (Prelims, Mains, and Interview) and optional subject coaching.

Dronacharya IAS Academy,

Dronacharya IAS Academy is another popular coaching center for civil services preparation in Maharashtra. It provides coaching for UPSC CSE as well as state-level civil services exams conducted by Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC). Dronacharya IAS Academy offers structured courses, individual attention, doubt-clearing sessions, and regular assessments to track progress.

Chanakya IAS Academy, Delhi

Chanakya IAS Academy is known for its holistic approach to civil services coaching, covering all aspects of the UPSC CSE syllabus. Provides coaching for GS papers, optional subjects, current affairs, essay writing, mock interviews, and personality development. Offers both classroom coaching and online programs, catering to the diverse needs of civil services aspirants.
