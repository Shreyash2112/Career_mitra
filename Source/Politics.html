Politics

Politics encompasses governance, power, and decision-making within society. It includes various systems like democracy and monarchy, political parties with distinct ideologies, and government branches (executive, legislative, judiciary). International relations, ideologies (liberalism, conservatism), and processes like elections and policymaking are key aspects. Political economy and philosophy also influence politics, with current issues including climate change, immigration, and human rights. Understanding politics involves studying history, law, economics, sociology, and international relations.
