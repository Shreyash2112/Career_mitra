Defence

Defense refers to safeguarding a nation's territory, people, and interests from external threats. It involves maintaining military forces like the Army, Navy, and Air Force, strategic planning to deter aggression, and ensuring national security across various domains. This includes cybersecurity, intelligence gathering, and diplomacy. The defense sector encompasses industries producing military equipment and technologies. Defense also extends to humanitarian aid, peacekeeping, and international relations, requiring strong civil-military relations and adherence to international law.

Top College

National Defence Academy (NDA), Khadakwasla

One of the most prestigious and sought-after options for students after 12th is joining the National Defence Academy (NDA). It is a joint services academy of the Indian Armed Forces, where cadets are trained for the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Indian Military Academy (IMA), Dehradun

For those interested in a career in the Indian Army, the Indian Military Academy (IMA) in Dehradun is a prominent choice. Candidates can join the IMA after completing their graduation through the Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE).

Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO), Hyderabad

For students interested in research and development in defense technologies, DRDO offers various opportunities through exams like the Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO) Entry Test (CEPTAM) and Scientist Entry Test (SET).
